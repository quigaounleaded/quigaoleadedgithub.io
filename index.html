Bedroom Design
For Adults

A bedroom should be comfy, cozy and, of course, stylish.
If your space is in need of a refresh, look no further.
Regardless of your design style — traditional, modern, rustic,
eclectic and more — this roundup of beautiful bedroom ideas can help amp up your next decorating plan and layout.

Design

Pair Black and Green for a Dramatic Retreat

A black and green color palette can be both elegant and casual.
Use green as your bed's backdrop and paint the surrounding bookshelves and accents,
like a wall sconce, a moody shade of black.

Try Metallic Accents

A neutral bedroom can benefit from eccentric accents like a metallic table lamp.
Here, the lamp pairs well with the blush tones in the space, offering a creative twist to an otherwise minimalist design.

Design an En Suite Bathroom

Play up the coziness of an en suite bathroom by installing white shiplap walls horizontally and outfitting the bedroom with a compact bed and nightstand to make the most of the space.

DIY Your Dresser

Blogger Emma upgraded her simple bedroom dresser using scrap paper, Plaid Royal Coat Decoupage and a blueprint copy of one of her favorite photos. It's a project you can try at home for less, as only five materials are needed.

Paint the Back of Your Bookcase

Energize your bedroom by adding a bold color to the back of your built-in bookcase. A vibrant orange or yellow, for instance, will instantly brighten a white room while matching any unexpected patterns, like the geometric design in this Greenwich Village room designed by Tilton Fenwick.

Include a Chic Bar Cart

Give your bedroom a glam look with a gold bar cart that can easily double as a nightstand. Choose one with just enough space to store your books, art, towels or baskets.

Reuse Arched Window Frames

Think twice before tossing away your old window frames. Rather than a traditional headboard, arched windows like these can bring character and a rustic flair into a bedroom.
